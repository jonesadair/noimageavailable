This project documents antiquities in the Metropolitan Museum of Art's collection that have no publicly available images in the Met's online database. Using the Met Collection API, it identifies and records these unpictured objects as a distinct subset of the collection, preserving their existing metadata, departmental context, chronology, and bibliographic references where present. The project operates as a parallel archive focused on digital absence within the Met's collection and supports the incremental addition of new visual documentation without altering the museum's original records.
